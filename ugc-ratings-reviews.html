About

As a Project Manager at Bazaarvoice, I led the implementation of the company's User Generated Content (UGC) platform for major global clients. UGC is a powerful tool for e-commerce, as it provides authentic customer testimonials, reviews, and social content that significantly impacts purchasing decisions.

I've worked with over 250 brands, including Air Wick, Bearpaw Shoes, Coca-Cola, Daring Foods, Dulcolax, e.l.f. Cosmetics, Fabletics, Goody Hair, Harley-Davidson, Jimmy Dean, Keurig, Kohl’s, Lay’s, Miracle-Gro, Nature's One, NYP Holdings, Party City Canada, QVC, Savage X Fenty, Shoe Carnival, Snapdragon, Tazo, Stok, True Religion, Wet n Wild, White Claw Zero, Whirlpool, 10 Chattem brands, and 22 P&G brands.

The implementation process involved:

  • Coordinating cross-functional teams to integrate Bazaarvoice's APIs and widgets into client websites
  • Managing client expectations and requirements throughout the implementation process
  • Creating scalable processes for onboarding new clients and ensuring successful platform adoption
  • Maintaining detailed documentation and tracking systems in JIRA, Slack, and Confluence
  • Troubleshooting technical issues and ensuring smooth deployment

Through systematic process improvements and careful project management, we were able to reduce production errors by 10%, cut operational costs by 20%, and contribute approximately $2 million in revenue growth for Bazaarvoice.

You can see many of these collaborations on the homepage of their websites—scroll to the bottom to view the UGC (User-Generated Content) gallery I helped create. Clicking into the gallery will take you to the product’s page, where the UGC is tagged. On the Product Detail Page (PDP), you'll also find the PDP gallery and Ratings & Reviews section.
